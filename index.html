Callanga: where the sky meets Earth

Mateo always dreamed of opening a restaurant to honor the food of his home country Ecuador. Callanga holds a special place in his heart, as it is his ancestors' hometown, surrounded by the magic of the Andes. His desire to share the food of his people, passed down through generations, is finally complete thanks to you.
